The Experience

Adobe has designed a digital experience where YOU get to remix the art you created throughout the week! Using the halftone technninque, combine your creations to inspire a new and unique piece of artwork. Halftone is defined as imagery create through the use of tiny dots or shapes (see the images in the gallery for an example). Select two of your own pieces or collaborate with other de Youngsters artists to create a new work of art!
